About MUN

Model United Nations (MUN) is an educational simulation where students role-play as delegates to the United Nations and simulate UN committees. It develops skills in diplomacy, negotiation, critical thinking, and public speaking.

MUN at The Indian Public School, Erode

MUN 4 Schools is an initiative by The Indian Public School, Erode, to bring the MUN experience to school students, fostering global citizenship and leadership.
